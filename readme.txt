This is a collection of publications, where individual submodels of the CSEM are described:

- Complete CSEM ---

A) Fichtner_GRL_2018.pdf: CSEM Generation I (comprising items 1 - 11 below)

- 1D background ---

1) Dziewonski_PEPI_1981.pdf: PREM, basis of the 1D background model

- 3D initial model ---

2) Ritsema_Science_1999.pdf: S20RTS, 3D S-velocity background model
3) Ritsema_GJI_2002.pdf: Scaling from S20RTS to P-velocity variations

- Initial crustal model ---

4) Meier_GJI_2007.pdf, Meier_GRL_2007.pdf: Initial crustal model

- Regional refinements ---

5) Rickers_EPSL_2013.pdf: North Atlantic
6) Colli_Tectonophysics_2013.pdf: South Atlantic
7) Fichtner_EPSL_2010.pdf: Australasia
8) Simute_JGR_2016.pdf: Japanese Islands
9) Fichtner_GJI_2013.pdf: Europe and western Asia
10) Cubuk-Sabuncu_PEPI_2017.pdf: Western Turkey
11) Fichtner_EPSL_2015.pdf: Western Mediterranean
12) Krischer_JGR_2018.pdf: North America and North Atlantic
